Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter, then whisk well.
  4. Pour into a greased baking tin and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool and serve with chocolate icing.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple (chopped)
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Wash and cut all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix fruits in a large bowl.
  3. Add honey and lemon juice, then toss gently.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.
