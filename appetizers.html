3. Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 10 large mushrooms
  • 50g cream cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp parsley

Steps:

  1. Remove mushroom stems.
  2. Mix cream cheese, garlic, and parsley.
  3. Fill mushroom caps with mixture.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes.

Appetizer Recipes

1. Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf French bread
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp parsley (chopped)

Steps:

  1. Cut bread into slices.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley.
  3. Spread mixture on bread slices.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 10 minutes.

2. Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices toasted bread
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Rub garlic on toasted bread.
  2. Mix tomatoes, olive oil, and basil.
  3. Spoon mixture onto bread slices.
  4. Serve fresh.
